ABOUT ME

My name is Avery Weksler-Hart, and I’m currently pursuing a degree in engineering at Brookdale Community College. I’ve always been passionate about understanding how things work and using creativity to solve problems, which naturally led me toward the engineering field. Outside of academics, I have a strong love for both art and horseback riding. Art allows me to express myself and explore different perspectives, while horseback riding has taught me patience, discipline, and the value of building trust with another being. During high school, I completed the Web Design Pathway Program at Marlboro High School, where I developed a solid foundation in digital design, front-end development, and project-based learning. I also passed the NOCTI exam, a national industry certification that further validated my technical skills and dedication to learning. I’m excited to continue growing both creatively and technically as I pursue a career that blends innovation, design, and real-world impact.
